About Us

Our Heritage & Vision

From Humble Beginnings to Global Presence

Founded in 2012 as Dream Waver Consultancy in Sylhet, Bangladesh, we began with a simple mission: to help individuals navigate the complex world of immigration with integrity and expertise.

Our 2016 rebranding to Eternal Associates marked our commitment to lasting relationships with clients. The 2018 expansion to Vancouver, Canada, positioned us uniquely to serve clients with on-the-ground expertise in North America.

Today, with dual-continent operations, we combine local knowledge with global perspective to deliver unparalleled immigration services.

Our Mission

To transform immigration challenges into success stories through ethical, personalized, and expert guidance.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted global immigration partner, recognized for excellence and client success.
